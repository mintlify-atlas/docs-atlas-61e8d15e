## Credit System Overview

Credits are the currency that powers photo generations in Cabina. As a partner, you:

1. **Purchase credits** in bulk at wholesale rates
2. **Allocate credits** to events or clients
3. **Monitor consumption** as users generate photos
4. **Top up** events or accounts as needed

## Partner Credit Balance

### Viewing Your Balance

Your total available credits are visible in multiple places:

- **Wallet Tab**: Primary credit management interface
- **Overview Tab**: Quick balance display in stats cards
- **Event Creation**: Shows available balance when allocating

**Balance Components:**
- **Credits Total**: Total purchased credits
- **Credits Used**: Sum of all allocated credits
- **Available**: Total - Used (your unallocated balance)

<Info>
**Real-Time Updates:** Your balance updates instantly when you allocate credits or make purchases. Refresh the page to ensure you're seeing the latest data.
</Info>

## Purchasing Partner Credits

### How to Buy Credits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Wallet">
    Click the **Wallet** tab in your Partner Dashboard header.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Current Balance">
    Your available credits display prominently at the top of the Wallet section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Cargar Créditos'">
    Tap the purchase button (usually green or prominent call-to-action).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Credit Package">
    Choose from available partner packages:
    
    | Package | Credits | Price (ARS) | Price per 1K |
    |---------|---------|-------------|-------------|
    | Starter | 10,000 | ~$8,000 | $800 |
    | Professional | 50,000 | ~$35,000 | $700 |
    | Business | 100,000 | ~$65,000 | $650 |
    | Enterprise | 250,000+ | Custom | $500-600 |
    
    <Note>
    Partner rates are discounted compared to end-user pricing. Contact sales for custom enterprise packages over 250,000 credits.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    - Redirected to Mercado Pago checkout
    - Choose payment method (credit card, bank transfer, etc.)
    - Complete transaction
    - Receive email confirmation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Credits Added Instantly">
    Upon payment approval:
    - Credits added to your partner balance immediately
    - Success toast notification appears
    - Balance updates in Wallet view
    - Transaction recorded in history
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bulk Purchase Discounts

Larger purchases offer better value:

- **10K credits**: Standard rate
- **50K+ credits**: ~12% discount
- **100K+ credits**: ~18% discount
- **250K+ credits**: ~25%+ discount (contact sales)

**Volume Calculation:**

If you run 10 events per month with 5,000 credits each:
- Monthly need: 50,000 credits
- Annual need: 600,000 credits
- **Recommendation**: Purchase 100K packages quarterly or 250K+ annually for maximum savings

## Allocating Credits

### To Events

When creating or topping up events:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or Edit Event">
    - **New Event**: Enter allocation in "Credits to Allocate" field
    - **Existing Event**: Click ShoppingCart icon in Events table
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Amount">
    Specify how many credits to allocate.
    
    **Validation:**
    - Cannot exceed your available balance
    - Must be positive number
    - Typical range: 500-50,000 per event
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Allocation">
    Credits transfer from your partner pool to the event's dedicated balance.
    
    **Effects:**
    - Your partner balance decreases
    - Event's allocated credits increase
    - Event can now be used by participants
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
**One-Way Transfer:** Once credits are allocated to an event, they cannot be returned to your partner pool. Only allocate what's needed.
</Warning>

### To Clients

For white-label resale to clients:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Clients Tab">
    Click **Clients** in dashboard header.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Client">
    Find the client card and click **"Asignar $"** (Assign Credits) button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credit Amount">
    In the Client Top-Up Modal:
    - Enter number of credits
    - View your available balance
    - Confirm transaction
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Client Receives Credits">
    - Credits transfer to client's account
    - Client can now allocate to their own events
    - You're billed from your partner balance
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Client Credit Flow:**

```
Partner Balance
  → Allocated to Client Account
    → Client allocates to their Events
      → End users consume for generations
```

<Info>
**Client Management:** This is useful for white-label scenarios where clients self-manage events but you control the credit supply.
</Info>

## Monitoring Credit Usage

### Wallet Dashboard

The Wallet tab provides comprehensive usage analytics:

**Key Metrics:**
- **Available Credits**: Your current allocable balance
- **Total Purchased**: Lifetime credit purchases
- **Total Allocated**: Sum across all events and clients
- **Usage Trend**: Graph showing consumption over time

**Transaction History:**
- Lists all credit purchases
- Shows allocations to events/clients

In the **Events** tab:

**Progress Bars:**
Each event shows credit consumption:
- **Green (0-75%)**: Healthy usage
- **Orange (75-90%)**: Monitor closely
- **Red (90-100%)**: Top up urgently

**Detailed View:**
Click on event to see:
- Total allocated
- Total used
- Remaining balance
- Average cost per generation
- Peak usage times

### Analytics Insights

**Understanding Your Data:**

- **Generations This Month**: Total photos created across all events
- **Average Credits Per Generation**: Helps estimate future needs
- **Most Active Events**: Identifies high-performing events
- **Style Popularity**: See which AI styles are most used

## Credit Optimization Strategies

### Accurate Allocation

**Formula:**

```
Event Credits = (Expected Guests × Photos Per Guest × Avg Cost) + Buffer

Example:
(150 guests × 3 photos × 100 credits) + 20% = 54,000 credits
```

**Buffer Guidelines:**
- Small events (under 50 guests): +30% buffer
- Medium events (50-200): +20% buffer
- Large events (200+): +15% buffer

### Bulk Purchasing

**When to Buy More:**

✅ **Buy in advance if:**
- You have 5+ events planned in next 30 days
- Current balance covers less than 2 weeks of typical usage
- Promotional pricing is available

❌ **Wait to buy if:**
- Uncertain about near-term event pipeline
- Testing the platform (start small)
- Cash flow constraints

### Cost Per Event Calculation

Track profitability:

```
Event Cost = (Credits Allocated × Your Cost Per Credit)
Event Revenue = (Price You Charge Client)
Profit Margin = ((Revenue - Cost) / Revenue) × 100

Example:
Allocated: 30,000 credits
Your cost: $0.65 per 1K = $19.50
You charge: $50.00
Margin: (($50 - $19.50) / $50) × 100 = 61%
```

### Preventing Waste

**Avoid Over-Allocation:**
- Start conservative, top up if needed
- Review past events for baseline estimates
- Use analytics to understand typical consumption

**Avoid Under-Allocation:**
- Running out mid-event creates bad experience
- Keep phone accessible during events to top up
- Set buffer of at least 15-20%

## Top-Up Workflows

### Emergency Top-Up During Event

If credits run low during an active event:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive Alert">
    Monitor dashboard or set up alerts (via support) for low balance.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open Events Tab">
    Navigate to Events view on mobile or desktop.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Shopping Cart Icon">
    On the event row, tap the green ShoppingCart button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Quick Top-Up">
    - Enter amount (e.g., 5,000-10,000)
    - Confirm from available balance
    - Credits added in seconds
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify">
    - Check progress bar updated
    - Event continues uninterrupted
    - Users can keep generating
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Best Practice:** Always have at least 20,000 unallocated credits in your partner balance as an emergency reserve.
</Info>

### Scheduled Top-Ups

For recurring events or clients:

1. Set calendar reminders to review usage weekly
2. Allocate credits in batches (monthly or per event)
3. Use analytics to predict needs
4. Maintain minimum balance threshold

## Credit Expiration & Policies

### Do Credits Expire?

**No.** Partner credits never expire:
- Purchased credits remain in account indefinitely
- Allocated credits stay with events permanently
- No time limit on usage

### Refund Policy

<Warning>
**Credits are Non-Refundable:**
- Once purchased, credits cannot be refunded
- Once allocated, credits cannot be unallocated
- Plan purchases carefully

**Exceptions:**
- Platform technical issues preventing usage
- Duplicate charges (refunded automatically)
- Account closure (contact support for evaluation)
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment succeeded but credits didn't arrive">
    **Steps:**
    1. Wait 5 minutes for processing
    2. Refresh the Wallet tab
    3. Check email for payment confirmation
    4. Check transaction history
    5. If still missing after 15 min, contact support with transaction ID
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't allocate credits to event">
    **Possible Causes:**
    - Insufficient partner balance (purchase more)
    - Event already deleted
    - Trying to allocate negative or zero amount
    - Typo in amount field
    
    **Solution:**
    - Verify available balance in Wallet
    - Ensure event exists in Events tab
    - Double-check amount entered
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Credits consumed faster than expected">
    **Investigate:**
    - Check Moderation view for event
    - Count actual generations vs. estimate
    - Review which styles were used (premium costs more)
    - Check for multiple generations per user
    
    **Adjust:**
    - Limit to standard styles only
    - Communicate usage limits to guests
    - Top up as needed
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Want to transfer credits between events">
    **Not Possible:**
    Credits cannot be moved between events once allocated.
    
    **Workaround:**
    - For future events, allocate conservatively
    - Top up from partner balance as needed
    - Over-allocated events can't return credits (plan carefully)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
