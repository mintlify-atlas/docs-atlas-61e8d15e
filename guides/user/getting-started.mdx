## Welcome to Cabina

Cabina is an AI-powered photo booth that transforms your selfies into stunning, professionally styled images. Whether you're at an event or using the platform independently, this guide will help you get started.

## Accessing Cabina

There are two main ways to access Cabina:

### At an Event

If you're at an event using Cabina:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scan the QR Code">
    Look for the QR code displayed at the event venue (usually on tables or at the photo booth station).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access the Event Portal">
    Scanning the QR code will take you directly to the event's custom Cabina interface at `app.metalabia.com/?event=[event-slug]`.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Event Branding">
    You'll see the event's custom logo, colors, and welcome message configured by the event organizer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Independent Access

For personal use outside of events:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Platform">
    Navigate to `app.metalabia.com` in your mobile browser.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Create an account or sign in using your email address.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Purchase Credits">
    Before generating photos, you'll need to purchase credits. See [Purchasing Credits](/guides/user/purchasing-credits) for details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Your Interface

Once you're logged in, you'll see:

- **Credits Display**: Shows your available credits in the top-right corner
- **Style Selector**: Browse available AI style packs to transform your photos
- **Camera Interface**: Access your device camera to capture or upload photos
- **Gallery Access**: View and download your previously generated images

## Your First Photo Generation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Style">
    Browse the style cards and tap one to select it. Selected styles show an orange accent border and display "ACTIVO" (Active).
    
    **Style indicators:**
    - Premium styles show a gold "Premium" badge
    - Popular styles display usage count badges
    - Top 5 trending styles have a sparkle animation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Take or Upload a Photo">
    - Tap the camera icon to take a new selfie
    - Or upload an existing photo from your device
    
    **Best practices:**
    - Ensure good lighting
    - Face the camera directly
    - Avoid wearing accessories that cover your face
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate Your Image">
    Tap the generate button. The AI will process your photo (this takes 30-60 seconds).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View and Download">
    Once complete, your transformed photo will appear. You can:
    - Download it to your device
    - Share it directly
    - View it in your gallery
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Credit Costs

Each photo generation consumes credits:

- **Standard Styles**: Varies per style (check the style card)
- **Premium Styles**: Higher credit cost, indicated with sparkle badges
- **Event Access**: If you're at an event, the organizer provides the credits

<Note>
At events, you don't need to worry about credits—they're provided by the event organizer. Just generate as many photos as you'd like!
</Note>

## Navigation Tips

- **Back Button** (ChevronLeft icon): Returns to previous screen
- **Settings Icon** (User icon): Access your profile and credit balance
- **Gallery Icon** (Image icon): View all your generated photos
- **Refresh Button**: Reload styles or update your gallery

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My photo generation failed">
    - Ensure you have sufficient credits
    - Check that your photo shows your face clearly
    - Try a different photo with better lighting
    - Refresh the page and try again
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I can't see any styles">
    - Refresh the page
    - Check your internet connection
    - If at an event, ensure the event is active
    - Contact the event organizer if styles seem limited
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="The interface looks different">
    Event organizers can customize the look with their own logos, colors, and welcome messages. This is normal!
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
