## Accessing Your Gallery

All your generated photos are automatically saved to your personal gallery.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Gallery">
    Tap the **Images icon** in the top navigation bar to access your full gallery.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Thumbnails">
    Your photos display in a grid layout:
    - Most recent photos appear first
    - Each thumbnail shows a preview
    - Hover/tap for quick action buttons
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Gallery Interface

### Layout Components

**Header Section:**
- 📸 Gallery icon
- Event name (if accessed via event)
- Photo count display
- Available credits counter
- Action buttons (Download All, Back)

**Photo Grid:**
- **Grid Layout**: 2-4 columns depending on screen size
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:5 portrait cards
- **Rounded Corners**: 24-32px border radius
- **Hover Effects**: Orange border and brightness increase on hover

**Stats Display:**
- **Total Photos**: Number of generations in gallery
- **Credits Remaining**: Available credit balance

## Viewing Individual Photos

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Photo">
    Tap any photo thumbnail in the grid.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Full-Screen Preview">
    The photo opens in a full-screen modal with:
    - High-resolution preview
    - Timestamp (when it was generated)
    - Action buttons
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Available Actions">
    While viewing a photo:
    - **Ver** (View): Opens full-size in new tab
    - **Download icon**: Saves to device
    - **X icon**: Closes and returns to gallery
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading Photos

### Download Single Photo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Photo">
    Tap the photo thumbnail in your gallery.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Download">
    In the overlay, tap the **Download icon** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Location">
    Your browser will prompt you to choose a save location, or auto-download to your Downloads folder.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**File Details:**
- **Format**: JPG
- **Quality**: High (90%+)
- **Naming**: `generacion-[index].jpg`

### Download All Photos

To download your entire gallery as a ZIP file:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Gallery View">
    Navigate to your full gallery using the Images icon.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Descargar Todo'">
    Find the "Download All" button in the header section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for ZIP Generation">
    - Status shows "Generando ZIP..." (Generating ZIP)
    - Processing time depends on photo count (2-10 seconds per 10 photos)
    - Don't close the browser during generation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save ZIP File">
    Once complete:
    - ZIP file downloads automatically
    - Filename: `event-photos-[event-slug].zip` or `fotos-[event-slug].zip`
    - Extract to view all photos
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**ZIP Contents:** All photos are numbered sequentially (generacion-1.jpg, generacion-2.jpg, etc.) for easy organization.
</Info>

## Event Gallery (Live View)

If you're accessing Cabina through an event, you may have access to the **Live Event Gallery**.

### Features

**Real-Time Updates:**
- Gallery auto-refreshes every 30 seconds
- New photos from all guests appear automatically
- Green pulse indicator shows auto-refresh status

**Event-Specific Display:**
- Event name header with 📸 icon
- Custom event branding (logo, colors)
- Shared gallery with all event participants

**Action Buttons:**
- **Volver** (Back): Return to camera view
- **Actualizar** (Refresh): Manual refresh with rotating icon

### Accessing Event Gallery

<Steps>
  <Step title="From Event Interface">
    When at an event, tap the "Gallery" or "Ver Galería" button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View All Event Photos">
    See photos generated by all event guests, ordered by most recent.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download Your Favorites">
    Tap any photo to view full-size and download.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**Privacy:** Event galleries show all participants' photos. Only download and share photos you have permission to use.
</Note>

## Gallery Organization

### Sorting and Filtering

Photos are automatically organized:

- **Default Sort**: Most recent first (descending by `created_at`)
- **Chronological Order**: Older photos appear at the bottom
- **Event Grouping**: If you've attended multiple events, photos may be grouped by event

### Finding Specific Photos

To locate a particular photo:

1. **Scroll Through Grid**: Recent photos appear at the top
2. **Check Timestamps**: Hover over photos to see creation time
3. **Filter by Event**: If available, use event dropdown to filter

<Info>
**Future Feature:** Advanced filtering by style, date range, and favorites is planned for future releases.
</Info>

## Managing Photos

### Deleting Photos

<Warning>
**Event Organizer Feature:** End users typically cannot delete photos. This feature is available to event organizers in their dashboard.
</Warning>

If you need a photo removed:
- Contact the event organizer
- Or contact Cabina support with the photo ID

### Storage Limits

Your gallery storage:
- **No Limit**: No cap on number of photos stored
- **Retention**: Photos are stored indefinitely
- **Access**: Always available while your account is active

## Mobile vs Desktop Experience

### Mobile View
- **2-column grid**: Optimized for phone screens
- **Touch gestures**: Swipe to close photo previews
- **Responsive images**: Optimized loading for mobile data
- **Native download**: Uses device's native download manager

### Desktop View
- **3-4 column grid**: More photos visible at once
- **Hover effects**: Better hover state feedback
- **Keyboard navigation**: Arrow keys to navigate photos
- **Direct download**: Browser's download manager

## Sharing from Gallery

### Social Media

To share a photo on social media:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Photo">
    Tap the photo thumbnail in your gallery.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download First">
    Download the photo to your device.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload to Platform">
    Open your social media app and upload from your device photos.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Direct social sharing from the gallery (via share button) may be available depending on your browser and device.
</Note>

### Via Link

Photos are stored at secure URLs:
- Each photo has a unique URL
- Right-click and "Copy Image Address" to share the link
- Links are permanent and publicly accessible

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Gallery not loading">
    **Solutions:**
    - Refresh the page
    - Check your internet connection
    - Clear browser cache
    - Sign out and sign back in
    - If persists, contact support
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Photos not appearing">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Generation is still processing (wait 60 seconds)
    - You're viewing the wrong event gallery
    - Photos were generated under a different account
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Tap the refresh button
    - Navigate back to main view and re-enter gallery
    - Verify you're signed in with the correct account
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Download fails">
    **Solutions:**
    - Check device storage (ensure sufficient space)
    - Try downloading one photo at a time
    - Use a different browser
    - For ZIP downloads, reduce batch size by downloading photos individually
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="ZIP file corrupted">
    **Solutions:**
    - Re-download the ZIP file
    - Try a different extraction tool (7-Zip, WinRAR)
    - Download photos individually if ZIP continues to fail
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Auto-refresh not working (events)">
    **Check:**
    - Look for green pulse indicator at bottom of gallery
    - Ensure you haven't navigated away from gallery view
    - Refresh manually using the Actualizar button
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Gallery Best Practices

### Regular Backups

- Download important photos immediately after generation
- Periodically download your full gallery as ZIP backup
- Store backups in cloud storage (Google Drive, iCloud, etc.)

### Organization Tips

- Rename downloaded photos with descriptive names
- Create folders on your device by event or date
- Tag favorites for easy identification

### Privacy Considerations

- Only share photos you have rights to distribute
- Be mindful when sharing event gallery photos (they may include other guests)
- Don't share direct image URLs publicly if they contain sensitive content
