## Understanding Credits

Credits are the currency used in Cabina to generate AI-powered photos. Each photo generation consumes a certain number of credits depending on the style complexity.

<Note>
**Event Users:** If you're accessing Cabina through an event QR code, credits are provided by the event organizer. You don't need to purchase credits!
</Note>

## Viewing Your Credit Balance

Your current credit balance is always visible:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the Header">
    Look at the top-right corner of the interface. You'll see a credits display with the Zap (⚡) icon.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Settings">
    Tap the Settings icon to view detailed credit information:
    - **Credits Available**: Your current balance
    - **Total Generations**: How many photos you've created
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Purchase Credits

### From the Settings View

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    Tap the User icon in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find the Credits Section">
    Scroll to the "Créditos y Consumo" (Credits and Usage) card with the orange gradient background.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap 'Cargar Más Créditos'">
    Click the "Load More Credits" button at the bottom of the credits card.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Package">
    Choose from available credit packages:
    - Starter packs (lower cost, fewer credits)
    - Value packs (best price per credit)
    - Premium packs (bulk discounts)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Follow the payment flow using Mercado Pago (for Argentina) or the configured payment processor.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    Once payment is approved, credits are added instantly to your account. You'll see a success toast notification.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### When You Run Out

If you attempt to generate a photo without sufficient credits:

1. You'll see an error message indicating insufficient credits
2. A prompt will appear offering to purchase more
3. Follow the same purchase flow described above

<Info>
Pricing may vary based on your region and current promotions. Check the payment modal for exact prices.
</Info>

## Understanding Credit Costs Per Generation

Different styles have different credit costs:

- **Standard Styles**: 50-100 credits per generation
- **Premium Styles**: 150-300 credits per generation (marked with sparkle badge)
- **Exclusive Styles**: Variable pricing based on complexity

### How to Check Style Costs

When browsing styles:
1. Premium styles show a gold "Premium" badge
2. The credit cost is displayed when you select a style
3. You'll see a confirmation before generating

## Payment Methods

### Supported Payment Options

- **Mercado Pago** (Argentina)
- **Credit/Debit Cards**
- **Bank Transfers** (for bulk purchases)

### Payment Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Secure Checkout">
    All payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Instant Credit Addition">
    Credits are added to your account immediately upon payment approval.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receipt via Email">
    You'll receive a confirmation email with transaction details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Credit Management Tips

### Monitor Your Usage

Track your credit consumption in the Settings view:
- View **Total Generations** to see how many photos you've created
- Monitor your **Credits Available** regularly
- Plan purchases based on your usage patterns

### Save Credits

- Preview styles before committing to a generation
- Use standard styles for casual photos
- Reserve premium styles for special occasions
- Download photos immediately (regenerating costs additional credits)

### Bulk Purchases

For heavy users:
- Larger packages offer better value per credit
- Consider premium packs if you generate 45+ photos per month
- Unused credits never expire

## Refund Policy

<Warning>
Credits are non-refundable once purchased. However, if you experience:
- Failed generations that consumed credits
- Technical errors during payment
- Duplicate charges

Contact support immediately for assistance.
</Warning>

## Transaction History

To view your credit purchase history:

1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Scroll to the **Credits and Usage** section
3. Your current balance shows recent activity

<Note>
For detailed transaction history, contact support with your account email.
</Note>

## Promotional Credits

Occasionally, you may receive bonus credits:
- **New User Bonus**: First-time sign-ups may receive starter credits
- **Referral Bonuses**: Invite friends for credit rewards
- **Event Promotions**: Special occasions may include credit gifts

These credits appear automatically in your balance.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment succeeded but credits didn't arrive">
    1. Refresh the page
    2. Sign out and sign back in
    3. Wait 5 minutes for processing
    4. Check your email for confirmation
    5. Contact support if credits don't appear within 15 minutes
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Payment failed">
    - Check your payment method details
    - Ensure sufficient funds
    - Try a different payment method
    - Clear browser cache and retry
    - Contact your bank if the issue persists
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="I was charged twice">
    - Check your email for duplicate confirmations
    - Verify your credit balance (may reflect both transactions)
    - Contact support immediately with transaction IDs
    - Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
