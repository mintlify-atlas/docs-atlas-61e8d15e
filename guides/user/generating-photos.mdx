## Overview

Cabina uses advanced AI to transform your photos into professionally styled images. This guide covers everything you need to create amazing results.

## Before You Start

Ensure you have:

- ✅ Sufficient credits in your account (or access via an event)
- ✅ A clear photo of yourself (good lighting, face visible)
- ✅ A stable internet connection

## The Photo Generation Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Your Style">
    Browse the available style cards displayed on the main interface.
    
    **Understanding Style Cards:**
    - Each card shows a preview image representing the style
    - Style name appears at the bottom of each card
    - Selected styles have an **orange accent border** and show "ACTIVO"
    - Unselected styles are dimmer and show "SELECCIONAR"
    
    **Style Badges:**
    - 🌟 **Premium Badge**: Gold badge in top-left corner (higher quality, costs more)
    - 📊 **Usage Counter**: Small badge showing "X usos" (usage count)
    - ✨ **Top Style**: Bouncing sparkle icon for top 5 trending styles
    - ❤️ **Favorite Button**: Heart icon in top-right (tap to favorite)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Capture or Upload Photo">
    After selecting a style:
    
    **Using Camera:**
    - Tap the camera icon to activate your device camera
    - Position yourself with good lighting
    - Face the camera directly
    - Tap the capture button (large circular button)
    
    **Uploading Existing Photo:**
    - Tap the upload icon
    - Select a photo from your device gallery
    - Ensure the photo shows your face clearly
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Generation">
    After capturing/uploading:
    - Review your photo preview
    - Verify the selected style
    - Check the credit cost display
    - Tap "Generate" to proceed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Processing">
    The AI generation takes 30-60 seconds:
    - You'll see a loading spinner with a progress indicator
    - Don't close the browser or navigate away
    - A toast notification appears when complete
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Result">
    Your generated photo appears:
    - Full-screen preview displays the result
    - Download button (Download icon) saves to your device
    - Share button allows social media sharing
    - "Generate Another" returns you to the style selector
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Style Selection Guide

### Browsing Styles

Styles are organized by categories:

- **Filter by Pack**: If at an event, you'll see only enabled packs
- **Search/Filter**: Use the Filter icon to narrow options
- **Favorites**: Tap the heart icon to mark favorites for quick access

### Understanding Style Types

#### Standard Styles
- No special badge
- Lower credit cost (50-100 credits)
- Fast processing
- Great for casual photos

#### Premium Styles
- Gold "Premium" badge with sparkle icon
- Higher credit cost (150-300 credits)
- Enhanced quality and detail
- Special artistic effects

#### Trending Styles
- Sparkle animation indicator
- Most popular among users
- Proven quality results
- Often featured in galleries

### Style Pack Categories

Common pack themes include:
- **Profesional**: Business and corporate styles
- **Artístico**: Artistic and creative effects
- **Vintage**: Retro and classic looks
- **Fantástico**: Fantasy and dramatic styles
- **Clásico**: Timeless, elegant portraits

<Info>
The available styles may be customized by event organizers. If you're at an event, you'll only see styles enabled for that specific event.
</Info>

## Photo Capture Best Practices

### Lighting Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Use natural light when possible
- Face the light source
- Avoid harsh shadows on your face
- Ensure even lighting across your face

❌ **Don't:**
- Use direct overhead lighting
- Backlight your photo (light behind you)
- Use colored lighting (causes unrealistic results)
- Generate in very dark environments

### Positioning Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Face the camera directly
- Fill the frame with your face and shoulders
- Keep a neutral or slight smile
- Remove glasses if possible (reduces glare)

❌ **Don't:**
- Turn at extreme angles
- Cover your face with hands or accessories
- Include multiple people (AI focuses on one face)
- Use blurry or low-resolution images

### Image Quality

- **Minimum Resolution**: 800x600 pixels
- **Recommended Resolution**: 1080x1080 or higher
- **Format**: JPG, PNG (avoid WebP or other formats)
- **File Size**: Under 10MB

## During Generation

### What's Happening

While the AI processes your photo:

1. **Upload** (5-10s): Your photo is securely uploaded
2. **Analysis** (10-20s): AI analyzes facial features
3. **Style Application** (20-30s): Style is applied
4. **Rendering** (10-15s): Final image is generated

### Progress Indicators

- **Spinner Animation**: Orange circular spinner indicates processing
- **Status Text**: May show "Generando..." (Generating)
- **No percentage**: The process doesn't show exact percentage

<Warning>
**Don't Refresh!** Refreshing or closing the page during generation will:
- Cancel the process
- Still consume your credits
- Require starting over
</Warning>

## After Generation

### Viewing Your Photo

Once complete:
- Photo displays in full-screen modal
- High-resolution preview
- Action buttons at the bottom:
  - **Download** (Download icon): Save to device
  - **Share** (ExternalLink icon): Open share menu
  - **Close** (X icon): Return to main view

### Downloading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Download Button">
    Click the download icon button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Location">
    Your browser will prompt for save location (or auto-download to Downloads folder).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Download">
    Check your Downloads folder or Photos app for the saved image.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**File Details:**
- Format: JPG
- Quality: High (90%)
- Filename: `generacion-[timestamp].jpg`

### Sharing

To share your generated photo:

1. Tap the share/external link button
2. Your device's native share menu appears
3. Select destination:
   - Social media (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp)
   - Email or messages
   - Save to cloud storage

## Generation Failed?

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Insufficient Credits">
    **Error:** "No tienes suficientes créditos"
    
    **Solution:**
    - Purchase more credits via Settings
    - Or wait if you're at an event (organizer may add more)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Face Not Detected">
    **Error:** "No se detectó un rostro válido"
    
    **Solution:**
    - Ensure your face is clearly visible
    - Use better lighting
    - Face the camera directly
    - Try a different photo
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Processing Timeout">
    **Error:** Generation takes longer than 2 minutes
    
    **Solution:**
    - Check your internet connection
    - Refresh the page
    - Try again
    - If repeated failures, contact support
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Poor Quality Result">
    **Issue:** Generated photo doesn't look good
    
    **Solution:**
    - Try a different style that suits your photo better
    - Use a higher quality source photo
    - Ensure better lighting in source photo
    - Premium styles generally produce better results
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Advanced Tips

### Maximize Quality

1. **Use High-Res Photos**: Higher input quality = better output
2. **Match Style to Photo**: Dramatic styles work best with dramatic poses
3. **Test Multiple Styles**: Try 2-3 styles to find the best match
4. **Consistent Lighting**: Even lighting produces more natural results

### Save Credits

- Preview style examples before generating
- Favorite your best styles for quick access
- Use standard styles for testing
- Reserve premium styles for final versions

### Batch Generation

For multiple photos in one session:

1. Prepare multiple source photos in advance
2. Select a style
3. Generate first photo
4. While viewing result, note the style if satisfied
5. Return and generate next photo with same/different style
6. All photos save to your gallery automatically
