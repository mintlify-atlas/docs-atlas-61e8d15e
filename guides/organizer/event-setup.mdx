## Role Overview

As an **Event Organizer** (also called "Client" in the system), you have a dedicated dashboard to customize and manage your specific event(s) created by your partner.

**Your Access:**
- Event-specific dashboard (not full partner dashboard)
- Customization controls for your event
- Gallery view of generated photos
- Credit usage monitoring

**Your Partner Controls:**
- Credit allocation
- Event creation and deletion
- Style pack availability
- Overall account settings

## Accessing Your Dashboard

Your partner will provide access to your Event Organizer dashboard.

### Initial Access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive Invitation Email">
    Your partner sends an invitation to your registered email address.
    
    **Email contains:**
    - Link to dashboard
    - Your login credentials
    - Event details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Click the link in email and sign in with your email address.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Event Dashboard Loads">
    You'll see your event's dedicated dashboard with:
    - Event name in header
    - Partner branding (unless overridden by you)
    - Configuration sections
    - Photo gallery
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**No Account Yet?** If you haven't received an invitation, contact your partner to ensure your email is correct and the invitation was sent.
</Note>

## Dashboard Overview

Your Event Organizer Dashboard includes:

### Header Section

- **Event Logo**: Your or partner's logo
- **Event Name**: Your event title
- **"Panel de Anfitrión" Badge**: Indicates Event Organizer view
- **Partner Attribution**: "Provisto por [Partner Name]" (if partner branding)
- **Event Status**: Green pulse "Evento Activo" indicator
- **Save Button**: Prominent "Guardar Cambios" (Save Changes)

### Main Sections

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Configuración General">
    **Basic Event Settings**
    - Event name
    - Event date and time
    - Welcome screen toggle
    - Welcome message text
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Identidad Visual">
    **Branding Customization**
    - Upload custom event logo
    - Select primary accent color
    - Preview branding
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Estilos Habilitados">
    **Available Styles** (Read-Only)
    - View styles enabled by partner
    - See contracted style packs
    - Cannot modify (contact partner to change)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Galería Live Feed">
    **Photo Gallery**
    - Real-time photo feed
    - Download individual photos
    - Download all photos as ZIP
    - Delete photos (if enabled)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Configuring Event Settings

### Event Name and Date

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find 'Configuración General'">
    Scroll to the first section labeled **"Configuración General"** with a Calendar icon.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Update Event Name">
    - Click the "Nombre del Evento" input field
    - Enter your event name (e.g., "Boda de Lucas & Martina")
    - This appears in header and gallery
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Event Date">
    - Click the date picker input
    - Custom calendar modal opens
    - Select date from calendar
    - Selected date shows in accent color
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Event Time">
    - Click the time picker input
    - Scrollable hour and minute columns appear
    - Select start time
    - Displays in 24-hour format
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Date/Time Usage:**
- Displays in welcome screen countdown
- Helps guests know event timing
- Doesn't restrict access (event is always active)

### Welcome Screen Configuration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Toggle Welcome Screen">
    In the "Configuración General" section:
    - Find "Pantalla de Bienvenida" toggle
    - Click switch to enable/disable
    - Enabled: Shows welcome screen before start time
    - Disabled: Users go straight to camera
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize Welcome Message">
    - Find "Mensaje de Bienvenida" textarea
    - Enter custom greeting (e.g., "¡Bienvenidos a nuestra fiesta! Hazte una foto mágica...")
    - Appears on welcome screen and camera interface
    - Keep under 200 characters for mobile display
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Welcome Message Tips:**

✅ **Good Examples:**
- "¡Bienvenidos a nuestra boda! Creá tu foto con IA y llevá un recuerdo mágico."
- "Celebremos juntos. Tomá una selfie y transformála con nuestros estilos exclusivos."
- "¡Gracias por estar aquí! Capturá este momento con inteligencia artificial."

❌ **Avoid:**
- Very long text (gets truncated on mobile)
- Technical instructions (keep it simple and inviting)
- Emoji overload (1-2 emojis are fine)

## Customizing Branding

### Upload Event Logo

Override your partner's logo with your own:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to 'Identidad Visual'">
    Find the section with a Palette icon.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Upload Box">
    - Click the dashed border upload area
    - Or click existing logo to replace
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Logo File">
    - Choose PNG or JPG (PNG with transparency recommended)
    - Max 5MB file size
    - Square or horizontal orientation works best
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Logo Uploads">
    - File uploads to storage
    - Preview appears immediately
    - Hover shows Edit icon to change
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Logo appears in:**
- Event kiosk header (top-left)
- QR code designs (when partner generates QR)
- Welcome screen

<Info>
**No Logo?** If you don't upload a custom logo, your partner's logo is used automatically.
</Info>

### Set Accent Color

Choose your event's primary color:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Color Swatches">
    In "Identidad Visual", find the "Color de Acento" grid.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Preset Color">
    - Click any of the 5 preset color swatches:
      - Purple (#7f13ec) - Elegant
      - Pink (#ec4899) - Romantic
      - Blue (#3b82f6) - Professional
      - Green (#10b981) - Fresh
      - Orange (#f59e0b) - Energetic
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Or Choose Custom Color">
    - Click the Plus icon swatch
    - Hidden color picker activates
    - Select any color
    - Or enter HEX code directly
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Preview Updates">
    - Selected swatch shows white border and ring
    - Color applies to date picker, buttons, and accents
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Color applies to:**
- Selected style card borders
- Date/time picker highlights
- Progress bars and loaders
- Active button states
- QR code accents

## Viewing Enabled Styles

Your partner controls which AI styles are available for your event.

### Estilos Habilitados Section

This section is **read-only** and shows:

- Grid of style thumbnails
- Style names below each thumbnail
- Number of styles included in your package

**If no specific styles:**
- Message shows "No hay estilos específicos contratados"
- Event uses partner's default style packs

**To change available styles:**
- Contact your partner
- Partner can enable/disable packs
- Partner can upgrade your style package

<Note>
**Style Selection:** When event guests use the kiosk, they choose from these enabled styles. You cannot add or remove styles yourself.
</Note>

## Managing Your Gallery

View and download all photos generated at your event.

### Accessing Gallery

In your dashboard, scroll to **"Galería Live Feed"** section.

**Gallery Features:**
- Horizontal scrollable photo grid
- Most recent photos appear first
- Thumbnail previews (aspect ratio 4:5)
- Hover shows action buttons

**Stats Display:**
- **Total Photos**: Number of generations
- **Unique Users**: Different guests who generated
- **Top Style**: Most popular style used

### Downloading Photos

#### Download Single Photo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hover Over Photo">
    Move mouse over photo thumbnail (or tap on mobile).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Overlay Appears">
    Gradient overlay with timestamp and buttons.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Download Button">
    Click the Download icon button.
    
    Photo downloads to your device immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### Download All Photos

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Descargar Todo'">
    In gallery header, click **"Descargar Todo"** (Download All).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="ZIP Generation">
    - Status shows "Generando ZIP..."
    - Wait while ZIP is created (5-30 seconds depending on photo count)
    - Don't close browser
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="ZIP Downloads">
    - File downloads automatically: `fotos-[event-slug].zip`
    - Extract ZIP to access all photos
    - Photos numbered sequentially: generacion-1.jpg, generacion-2.jpg, etc.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Large Events:** For events with 100+ photos, ZIP generation may take 1-2 minutes. Be patient and don't refresh the page.
</Info>

### Deleting Photos

<Warning>
**Check Permissions:** Photo deletion may be restricted depending on your partner's settings. If you don't see a delete button, contact your partner.
</Warning>

If deletion is enabled:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hover Over Photo">
    Find the photo you want to delete.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Trash Icon">
    Trash2 icon appears in overlay (red button).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    - Confirmation dialog appears
    - Click "Confirmar" to delete
    - Action is permanent
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Photo Removed">
    Photo disappears from gallery immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**When to Delete:**
- Inappropriate content
- Duplicate generations
- Poor quality results
- Guest requests removal

## Monitoring Credit Usage

Track how many credits your event has consumed.

### Credit Display

Not prominently displayed in Event Organizer view (partner manages this).

**If you need to check:**
- Contact your partner
- Partner can view usage in their Events tab
- Partner tops up if running low

<Note>
**Running Out?** If guests report errors generating photos, you may be out of credits. Contact your partner to add more.
</Note>

## Saving Your Changes

After making any customizations:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Changes">
    Double-check event name, date, branding, and messages.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Guardar Cambios'">
    In the top-right corner, click the large purple **"Guardar Cambios"** (Save Changes) button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Save">
    - Button shows loading spinner
    - Text changes to "Guardando..."
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    - Success toast: "¡Configuración guardada con éxito! ✨"
    - Changes apply immediately
    - Event kiosk updates for new users
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Existing Users:** Guests currently on the kiosk page will see updates when they refresh or reload the page.
</Info>

## Testing Your Event

Before sharing with guests, test the full experience:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Event URL">
    Ask your partner for the event URL:
    `app.metalabia.com/?event=[your-event-slug]`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open on Mobile Device">
    Use your smartphone to access the URL (most guests will use mobile).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Branding">
    - Logo appears correctly
    - Colors match your selection
    - Welcome message displays properly
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test Photo Generation">
    - Select a style
    - Take a selfie
    - Generate photo
    - Verify result quality
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Gallery">
    - Return to your dashboard
    - Refresh gallery section
    - Confirm test photo appears
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can't access dashboard">
    **Solution:**
    - Check email for invitation link
    - Ensure you're using correct email address
    - Check spam folder for invitation
    - Contact partner to resend invitation
    - Verify event is associated with your email
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Changes not saving">
    **Checks:**
    - Ensure you clicked "Guardar Cambios" button
    - Wait for success toast confirmation
    - Check internet connection
    - Try refreshing page and re-entering changes
    - Contact partner if issue persists
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Logo not uploading">
    **Solution:**
    - Check file size (max 5MB)
    - Use PNG or JPG format
    - Try a different image
    - Ensure stable internet connection
    - Try different browser (Chrome recommended)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Gallery photos not loading">
    **Solution:**
    - Refresh the page
    - Check that event is active
    - Verify photos are being generated (ask guests)
    - Contact partner to check event status
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Guests can't access event">
    **Checks:**
    - Verify event URL is correct
    - Ensure event start date hasn't passed (if set)
    - Check with partner that event has credits
    - Confirm event is active (not paused)
    - Have partner verify event status
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

### Before Event Launch
- ✅ Customize all branding at least 24 hours before event
- ✅ Test event URL on multiple devices
- ✅ Save contact info for partner (in case of issues)
- ✅ Download QR codes from partner (for printing)

### During Event
- 👀 Monitor gallery periodically
- 📱 Keep phone accessible to check dashboard
- 👮‍♀️ Delete inappropriate content promptly
- 📸 Encourage guests to share their photos

### After Event
- 📥 Download all photos as backup
- 📧 Share gallery link with guests (if enabled)
- 📊 Review which styles were most popular
- 📝 Provide feedback to partner
