## Live Gallery Overview

The Live Gallery is your real-time view of all photos being generated at your event. As guests create AI photos, they appear instantly in your dashboard.

## Accessing the Live Gallery

### From Your Event Organizer Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In to Dashboard">
    Access your Event Organizer dashboard at the link provided by your partner.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Scroll to Gallery Section">
    Find the **"Galería Live Feed"** section (ImageIcon) in your dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Photo Stream">
    Photos display in a horizontal scrollable grid:
    - Most recent photos on the left
    - Thumbnails with aspect ratio 4:5
    - Hover/tap for actions
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Gallery Stats

At the top of the gallery section, you'll see:

- **Total Photos**: Count of all generated photos
- **Unique Users**: Number of different guests (if tracked)
- **Top Style**: Most popular AI style used

<Info>
**Auto-Refresh:** The gallery updates automatically as new photos are generated. No need to refresh the page manually.
</Info>

## Gallery Interface

### Photo Grid

**Layout:**
- Horizontal scroll (left-right)
- Rounded corners (24-32px radius)
- Border on hover (white/accent color)
- Smooth transitions and animations

**Photo Cards Show:**
- Thumbnail preview
- Timestamp (on hover)
- Action buttons (on hover)
- Generation style (if labeled)

### Action Buttons

Hover over any photo to reveal:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Ver (View)">
    **Opens full-size photo**
    - Click to view high-resolution version
    - Opens in new modal or tab
    - Allows detailed inspection
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Download">
    **Download single photo**
    - Saves photo to your device
    - High-quality JPG format
    - Filename: `generacion-[index].jpg`
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Delete (if enabled)">
    **Remove photo from gallery**
    - Trash icon button
    - Requires confirmation
    - Permanent deletion
    - Use for inappropriate content
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Monitoring During Events

### Real-Time View

As your event progresses:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Keep Dashboard Open">
    Open your dashboard on a laptop, tablet, or phone.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Watch for New Photos">
    - New photos appear on the left
    - Smooth animation as they load
    - Scroll left to see most recent
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Quick Review">
    - Glance at thumbnails periodically
    - Check for inappropriate content
    - Monitor overall quality
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What to Monitor

✅ **Look for:**
- Photo quality and results
- Guest engagement (are photos being generated?)
- Popular styles (which are used most)
- Any technical issues (failed generations)

🚩 **Watch out for:**
- Inappropriate content (explicit, offensive)
- Duplicate spam generations
- Poor quality results (report to partner)
- Signs of credit depletion (generations stop)

## Downloading Photos

### Download Individual Photos

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hover Over Photo">
    Move mouse over thumbnail (or tap on mobile).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Download Icon">
    Click the Download button in the overlay.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save to Device">
    Photo downloads immediately as JPG file.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**When to Download Individually:**
- Showcase photos for social media
- Send specific photo to guest who requests it
- Save particularly good results

### Download All Photos (Bulk)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Descargar Todo'">
    In the gallery header, find and click **"Descargar Todo"** (Download All) button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for ZIP Generation">
    - Progress indicator shows "Generando ZIP..."
    - Takes 5-30 seconds depending on photo count
    - Don't close browser during process
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="ZIP Downloads Automatically">
    - File downloads: `fotos-[event-slug].zip` or `event-photos-[slug].zip`
    - Extract ZIP to access all photos
    - Photos numbered: generacion-1.jpg, generacion-2.jpg, etc.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**When to Download All:**
- End of event (backup)
- Sharing full gallery with client/partner
- Creating recap video or slideshow
- Archival purposes

<Note>
**Large Events:** For events with 100+ photos, ZIP generation can take 1-2 minutes. Be patient and don't refresh.
</Note>

## Moderating Content

### Deleting Inappropriate Photos

<Warning>
**Check Your Permissions:** Photo deletion may not be available depending on your partner's configuration. If you don't see delete buttons, contact your partner.
</Warning>

If deletion is enabled:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify Inappropriate Photo">
    Scan gallery for content that violates guidelines:
    - Explicit or adult content
    - Offensive gestures or symbols
    - Harassment or bullying
    - Copyright violations
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Delete Button">
    Hover over photo and click Trash icon (usually red).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    - Confirmation dialog appears: "¿Estás seguro de eliminar esta foto?"
    - Click "Confirmar"
    - Action is permanent and cannot be undone
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Photo Removed">
    - Photo disappears from gallery immediately
    - No longer accessible to guests
    - Cannot be recovered
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Moderation Guidelines

**Delete if photo contains:**
- ❌ Nudity or sexually explicit content
- ❌ Hate speech or offensive symbols
- ❌ Harassment of identifiable individuals
- ❌ Illegal activity
- ❌ Spam or test generations (if excessive)

**Keep if photo:**
- ✅ Is slightly blurry but appropriate
- ✅ Has unusual but not offensive style result
- ✅ Duplicates (unless excessive spam)
- ✅ Low quality but harmless

<Info>
**When in Doubt:** If unsure whether to delete, contact your partner or keep the photo. Over-moderation can frustrate guests.
</Info>

### Bulk Moderation (Partner Feature)

Your partner may have access to advanced moderation:

- Bulk select multiple photos
- Delete in batches
- Filter by date/user
- Search functionality

If you need bulk actions, request your partner access their **Moderation Section** for your event.

## Sharing the Gallery

### Gallery Link for Guests

Some configurations allow public gallery access:

**If Enabled:**
- Gallery is accessible at: `photobooth.metalabia.com/?event=[slug]` or similar
- Guests can view (and download) all event photos
- No generation capability, view-only

**Sharing:**
1. Ask your partner for the gallery URL
2. Share link via email, social media, event website
3. Guests can revisit after event to download photos

<Note>
**Privacy Consideration:** Public gallery means all guests see everyone's photos. Ensure this is appropriate for your event type.
</Note>

### Private Gallery (Dashboard Only)

If gallery is not public:

- Only you (Event Organizer) can see photos via dashboard
- Download all and share ZIP file with guests after event
- Or share individual photos upon request

## Live Projection/Slideshow

For added engagement, display the live gallery at your event:

### Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboard on Display Device">
    Use a laptop connected to projector or large TV screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Gallery">
    Scroll to Galería Live Feed section.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Full-Screen Browser (Optional)">
    Press F11 to full-screen browser for cleaner display.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Scroll or Slideshow">
    - If available, click slideshow mode button
    - Photos auto-advance every 5-10 seconds
    - Or manually scroll to show newest photos
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Benefits:**
- Guests see their photos appear in real-time
- Encourages more participation
- Creates social buzz and excitement
- Guests share photos of the projection

<Info>
**Technical Note:** If your dashboard doesn't have a built-in slideshow mode, you can manually refresh the gallery section periodically to show new photos.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Gallery not updating with new photos">
    **Solutions:**
    - Refresh your browser page
    - Check internet connection
    - Verify event is active (ask partner)
    - Ensure guests are actually generating photos (test yourself)
    - If dashboard is open for hours, reload page
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Photos loading slowly or not displaying">
    **Possible Causes:**
    - Slow internet connection
    - High volume of photos (100+ loads slowly)
    - Image hosting issue
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Switch to faster WiFi network
    - Try a different browser
    - Close other browser tabs
    - Contact partner if persistent
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can't download ZIP file">
    **Checks:**
    - Ensure sufficient device storage
    - Check browser's download settings
    - Disable pop-up blockers
    - Try different browser
    
    **Workaround:**
    - Download photos individually (slower but works)
    - Ask partner to download from their side and share
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Delete button not visible">
    **Cause:**
    Partner has not enabled deletion for Event Organizers.
    
    **Solution:**
    - Contact partner to enable deletion permissions
    - Or request partner delete specific photos for you
    - Provide photo timestamp or description
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Gallery shows photos from different events">
    **Issue:**
    Rare bug or incorrect event association.
    
    **Solution:**
    - Verify you're signed in with correct account
    - Check event name in dashboard header
    - Contact partner to verify event configuration
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Analytics & Insights

### Understanding Gallery Stats

**Total Photos:**
- Count of all successful generations
- Includes all guests' photos
- Use to measure event engagement

**Unique Users:**
- Number of different devices/sessions
- May not be 100% accurate (incognito mode, etc.)
- Gives rough estimate of reach

**Top Style:**
- Most frequently selected AI style
- Helps understand guest preferences
- Inform future event style selections

### Post-Event Analysis

**Questions to Ask:**
- How many photos per guest on average? (Total Photos ÷ Guest Count)
- Which styles were most popular?
- What time had peak usage?
- Any quality issues?

**Use Insights For:**
- Future event planning
- Credit allocation estimates
- Style pack selection
- Venue setup improvements

## Best Practices

### During Event
- 💻 Keep dashboard open on dedicated device
- 👀 Check gallery every 15-30 minutes
- 👮‍♀️ Moderate quickly if inappropriate content appears
- 🎉 Celebrate milestones ("100 photos!") to encourage participation

### After Event
- 📥 Download full ZIP as soon as possible
- 💾 Backup ZIP to cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox)
- 📧 Share gallery link or ZIP with guests within 24-48 hours
- 📊 Review stats and gather feedback

### Privacy & Respect
- 🔒 Only share photos publicly if guests were informed
- 📸 Respect deletion requests from guests
- ⚠️ Moderate objectively (don't delete just because you dislike result)
- ✅ Keep downloaded photos secure
