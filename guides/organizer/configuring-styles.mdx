## Understanding AI Styles

AI styles transform photos using different artistic effects, filters, and transformations. Each style creates a unique look, from professional portraits to fantasy art.

## Styles vs. Style Packs

### Style Packs (Subcategories)

Styles are organized into packs based on theme:

| Pack Name | Description | Best For |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Profesional** | Business portraits, LinkedIn-style, corporate | Corporate events, conferences, headshots |
| **Artístico** | Creative, artistic, painterly effects | Weddings, creative events, art shows |
| **Vintage** | Retro, classic, nostalgic filters | Themed parties, vintage weddings |
| **Fantástico** | Fantasy, dramatic, cinematic styles | Themed events, entertainment |
| **Clásico** | Timeless, elegant, traditional portraits | Formal events, galas, elegant affairs |

### Individual Styles

Within each pack, there are multiple individual styles. For example:

**Profesional Pack might include:**
- Corporate Headshot
- Studio Portrait
- Natural Light
- Executive Style

**Artístico Pack might include:**
- Watercolor
- Oil Painting
- Sketch Art
- Abstract

<Info>
Specific style names and counts depend on your partner's configuration and the platform's available IDENTITIES.
</Info>

## Who Controls Styles?

### Partner Level

Your **Partner** controls:
- Which style packs are available to you
- Default styles for all their events
- Access to premium vs. standard styles

### Event Organizer Level (You)

As an **Event Organizer**, you:
- View which packs are enabled (read-only in dashboard)
- Cannot add/remove packs yourself
- Can request changes from your partner

### When Creating Events

Your partner can:
- Select specific packs per event
- Override default pack selections
- Customize style availability for your event

## Viewing Your Enabled Styles

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Your Dashboard">
    Sign in to your Event Organizer dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Find 'Estilos Habilitados'">
    Scroll to the **"Estilos Habilitados"** section (Zap icon).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Style Grid">
    You'll see:
    - Thumbnail preview of each style
    - Style name below thumbnail
    - Total count of enabled styles
  </Step>
</Steps>

**If you see:**
- **Grid of styles**: These are your contracted styles
- **"No hay estilos específicos contratados"**: You're using partner's default pack selection

<Note>
**Partner Defaults:** If no specific styles are shown, your event uses your partner's enabled style packs. This is common and perfectly fine—your guests will still have plenty of options.
</Note>

## Requesting Style Changes

Since you cannot modify styles yourself, communicate with your partner:

### When to Request Changes

**Add Styles:**
- Your event theme requires specific aesthetic (e.g., vintage wedding needs Vintage pack)
- Guests expect more variety
- Competitor offerings include styles you don't have

**Remove Styles:**
- Too many options confuse guests
- Certain styles don't fit your event (e.g., fantasy styles at corporate event)
- Quality issues with specific styles

### How to Request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Document Your Request">
    Note which packs you want added/removed and why:
    - Event type and audience
    - Specific packs needed
    - Examples of desired aesthetic
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Contact Your Partner">
    Email or call your partner with:
    - Event name and date
    - Current style situation
    - Requested changes
    - Justification (optional but helpful)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Partner Updates Settings">
    Your partner:
    - Updates style packs in their dashboard
    - Saves changes
    - Notifies you when complete
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Changes">
    - Refresh your dashboard
    - Or test the event URL
    - Confirm new styles appear
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Timing:** Request style changes at least 48 hours before your event to allow time for testing and adjustments.
</Info>

## Style Selection Strategy

### Match Event Type

| Event Type | Recommended Packs | Avoid |
|------------|-------------------|-------|
| Corporate Event | Profesional, Clásico | Fantástico |
| Wedding | Artístico, Vintage, Clásico | - |
| Birthday Party | All packs (variety) | - |
| Conference | Profesional only | Artístico, Fantástico |
| Brand Activation | Artístico, Custom | - |
| Themed Party | Match theme (e.g., Vintage for 80s party) | Unrelated styles |

### Consider Your Audience

**Professional Audience:**
- Enable Profesional and Clásico
- Results are LinkedIn-ready
- Guests can use for business purposes

**Creative Audience:**
- Enable Artístico and Fantástico
- Guests want shareable, unique content
- Variety encourages multiple generations

**General/Mixed Audience:**
- Enable 3-4 packs for variety
- Include at least one "safe" option (Profesional or Clásico)
- Balance creative and traditional

### Optimize for Sharing

**For Social Media:**
- Artístico styles are highly shareable
- Fantástico creates wow-factor
- Bold, creative styles drive engagement

**For Keepsakes:**
- Clásico produces timeless results
- Guests will want to keep and print
- Professional quality

## Premium vs. Standard Styles

Some styles may be marked as **Premium** (gold badge with sparkle).

### Differences

| Aspect | Standard Styles | Premium Styles |
|--------|----------------|----------------|
| **Quality** | High quality | Enhanced quality |
| **Detail** | Good detail | More refined details |
| **Credit Cost** | 50-100 credits | 150-300 credits |
| **Processing** | 30-45 seconds | 45-60 seconds |
| **Use Case** | General use | Special photos |

### Availability

- Premium styles may require higher-tier partnership
- Your partner controls premium access
- Not all partners offer premium styles

<Note>
**Credit Impact:** Premium styles consume more credits per generation. Discuss with your partner if budget is a concern.
</Note>

## How Guests See Styles

When guests access your event kiosk:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Style Selection Screen">
    Guests see a grid of style cards:
    - Thumbnail preview image
    - Style name
    - Selection indicator
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Style Indicators">
    Cards show:
    - **Premium Badge**: Gold badge for premium styles
    - **Usage Counter**: Shows how many times used (if popular)
    - **Top Style Badge**: Sparkle icon for top 5 trending
    - **Favorite Heart**: Guests can favorite styles (if enabled)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Selection">
    - Tap card to select
    - Selected card shows orange border and "ACTIVO" label
    - Only one style active at a time
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generation">
    - Guest takes photo
    - AI applies selected style
    - Result shows transformed image
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Mobile Experience

Most guests use smartphones:
- Styles display in 2-column grid on mobile
- Scrollable if many styles (vertical scroll)
- Touch-friendly tap targets
- Preview images load quickly

<Info>
**Testing Recommended:** View the event URL on your phone to see the guest experience firsthand.
</Info>

## Style Performance Insights

After your event, you can see which styles were most popular.

### In Your Dashboard

**Gallery Stats Show:**
- **Top Style**: The most used style at your event
- Helps understand guest preferences
- Inform future style selections

### Partner Analytics

Your partner can provide more detailed analytics:
- Usage count per style
- Which styles were never used
- Peak usage times per style
- Quality ratings (if feedback is collected)

**Request Analytics:**
- Contact partner after event
- Ask for style usage report
- Use data to optimize future events

## Common Style Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Corporate Headshot Event

**Goal:** Professional headshots for LinkedIn

**Recommended Setup:**
- **Enable**: Profesional pack only
- **Why**: Consistent, professional results
- **Guest Count**: Usually 50-200
- **Credit Estimate**: 100 credits × 1-2 photos each = 10,000-20,000 credits

### Scenario 2: Wedding Reception

**Goal:** Fun, shareable memories

**Recommended Setup:**
- **Enable**: Artístico, Vintage, Clásico
- **Why**: Variety for different tastes; romantic aesthetic
- **Guest Count**: 100-150
- **Credit Estimate**: 100 credits × 3 photos each = 30,000-45,000 credits

### Scenario 3: Festival/Public Event

**Goal**: Maximum engagement, social sharing

**Recommended Setup:**
- **Enable**: All packs (5-6 packs)
- **Why**: Variety drives multiple generations; wow factor
- **Guest Count**: 500+
- **Credit Estimate**: 100 credits × 2 photos × 500 guests = 100,000+ credits

### Scenario 4: Themed Party (e.g., 1920s Gatsby)

**Goal**: Match event theme

**Recommended Setup:**
- **Enable**: Vintage, Clásico
- **Why**: Matches 1920s aesthetic; cohesive experience
- **Guest Count**: 50-80
- **Credit Estimate**: 100 credits × 2-3 photos = 10,000-15,000 credits

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Guests say no styles are available">
    **Checks:**
    - Verify at least one pack is enabled (check dashboard)
    - Contact partner to confirm event has styles selected
    - Have guest refresh page
    - Test URL yourself on mobile
    
    **Solution:**
    - Partner may need to edit event and select style packs
    - Ensure event was created with styles selected
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Specific style not showing for guests">
    **Possible Causes:**
    - Style is part of a disabled pack
    - Partner hasn't enabled that style
    - Style was removed from platform (deprecated)
    
    **Solution:**
    - Confirm style name with partner
    - Partner checks Branding > Estilos IA settings
    - May need to re-enable pack containing that style
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Too many styles, guests feel overwhelmed">
    **Solution:**
    - Request partner reduce to 2-3 packs
    - Focus on event-appropriate styles
    - Provide guidance to guests (signage suggesting top 3 styles)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Style quality is poor">
    **Investigation:**
    - Note which specific style produces poor results
    - Check if issue is consistent or photo-specific
    - Test with different source photos
    
    **Action:**
    - Report to partner with examples
    - Partner can contact support to flag style issues
    - May disable problematic style temporarily
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Guests complaining about credit cost">
    **If using premium styles heavily:**
    - Premium styles cost 2-3x standard styles
    - May deplete event credits faster
    
    **Solution:**
    - Ask partner to add more credits
    - Or request to disable premium-only packs
    - Balance premium and standard options
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

### Pre-Event
- 🎯 Test all enabled styles with your own photo
- 📝 Document which styles work best
- 📢 Brief guests on recommended styles (signage/announcements)
- 👥 Align style selection with event aesthetic

### During Event
- 👀 Monitor which styles are popular
- 💬 Collect informal feedback from guests
- 👮‍♂️ Watch for any quality issues
- 📊 Track if certain styles are underused

### Post-Event
- 📊 Request usage analytics from partner
- 📝 Note what worked and what didn't
- 💬 Share feedback with partner
- 🔁 Apply learnings to next event
