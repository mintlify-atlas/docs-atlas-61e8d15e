## QR Code Overview

QR codes provide a simple way for event guests to access your Cabina photo booth instantly by scanning with their smartphone cameras.

## How QR Codes Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Guest Scans QR Code">
    Using their phone's camera app or QR scanner.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Redirects to Event URL">
    QR code contains your event's unique URL:
    `app.metalabia.com/?event=[your-event-slug]`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Event Kiosk Loads">
    Guest sees your branded photo booth interface immediately.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Guest Generates Photo">
    Selects style, takes photo, generates AI image.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**No App Install Required:** Guests don't need to download any app. The web-based kiosk works directly in their mobile browser.
</Info>

## Getting Your QR Code

### Partner Generates QR Codes

<Note>
**Important:** Event Organizers (you) cannot generate QR codes directly. Your partner creates them for you.
</Note>

**To Get Your QR Code:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact Your Partner">
    Email or call your partner and request QR code.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Partner Generates in Dashboard">
    Partner accesses their dashboard, clicks on your event, and generates the QR code.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Partner Sends QR File">
    Partner downloads the QR image and sends it to you via email or file sharing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### What's Included in QR Design

The generated QR code includes:

- **Event Name**: Your event title at the top
- **Custom Text**: "📸 Escaneá y creá tu foto con IA"
- **QR Code**: Scannable code in center (white background)
- **Event Logo**: Your custom logo or partner logo (if configured)
- **Brand Color**: Accent color from branding settings
- **Footer**: "Powered by MetaLab IA • metalab30.com"

**File Details:**
- **Format**: PNG image
- **Dimensions**: 520px × ~700px (optimized for print and digital)
- **Resolution**: High-res for printing
- **Filename**: `qr-[event-slug].png`

## Printing QR Codes

### Print Specifications

**Recommended Print Sizes:**
- **Small (Flyers, Cards)**: 3" × 4" minimum
- **Medium (Table Tents)**: 5" × 7"
- **Large (Posters, Signage)**: 8" × 11" or 11" × 17"

**Print Quality:**
- Use high-quality printer (300 DPI+)
- Print on matte or semi-gloss cardstock
- Ensure QR code is sharp and clear
- Test scan before mass printing

<Warning>
**Always Test:** Print one copy first and scan with multiple phones to ensure it works before printing hundreds.
</Warning>

### Print Formats

#### Table Tents

**Use Case:** Place on guest tables at receptions

**Setup:**
1. Print QR on cardstock (8.5" × 5.5" folded in half)
2. Add text: "Scan to create your AI photo!"
3. Place on each table

**Quantity Needed:**
- 1 per table (or 1 per 2 tables for large venues)

#### Posters

**Use Case:** Entry points, photo booth area

**Setup:**
1. Print on 11" × 17" poster paper
2. Include instructions: "1. Scan QR, 2. Choose Style, 3. Take Selfie"
3. Mount on easel or wall

**Quantity Needed:**
- 2-4 posters (entrance, bar, photo area, exit)

#### Handheld Cards

**Use Case:** Roaming staff to encourage participation

**Setup:**
1. Print on business card size (3.5" × 2" or 4" × 3")
2. Laminate for durability
3. Give to event staff/volunteers

**Quantity Needed:**
- 5-10 cards for staff

### Professional Printing Services

For large or high-profile events:

- **Vistaprint**: Upload PNG, order table tents or posters
- **Local Print Shops**: Higher quality, faster turnaround
- **Online Services**: Moo, GotPrint, Printful

**Tips:**
- Provide high-res PNG file
- Request proof before full run
- Order 10% extra for backups

## Placement Strategies

### High-Traffic Areas

✅ **Best Locations:**
- **Entrance/Registration**: First thing guests see
- **Guest Tables**: Guests sitting have time to scan
- **Bar Area**: While waiting for drinks
- **Photo Booth Zone**: Dedicated area with instructions
- **Restroom Entrance**: Captive audience while waiting

❌ **Avoid:**
- Dance floor (too chaotic)
- Dark corners (scanning issues)
- Behind obstacles
- Too high or too low (eye level is best)

### Venue-Specific Tips

**Weddings:**
- Table tents on each guest table
- Poster at entrance
- Card in favor bags

**Corporate Events:**
- Poster at registration desk
- Flyers in welcome packets
- Digital slide in presentation deck

**Conferences:**
- QR on name badge lanyards
- Poster at booth
- Slide in speaker presentations

**Festivals/Outdoor:**
- Large banners at multiple entry points
- Staff with handheld cards
- Projected on screens

## Using Direct URL Instead of QR

If QR codes aren't feasible, share the direct URL:

**Your Event URL:**
```
app.metalabia.com/?event=[your-event-slug]
```

**Where to Use:**
- Text messages to guests
- Social media posts
- Email signatures
- Verbal announcements ("Visit app dot metalabia dot com slash events slash [slug]")

**Pros of URL:**
- Works in all contexts (digital and verbal)
- Can be bookmarked
- Easy to type

**Cons of URL:**
- Longer than scanning QR
- Risk of typos
- Less immediate than scan

<Info>
**Best Practice:** Use QR codes for in-person events, and direct URLs for digital promotion.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="QR code won't scan">
    **Possible Causes:**
    - Print quality too low (blurry QR)
    - QR code too small
    - Poor lighting at venue
    - Guest's camera not focused
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Reprint at higher resolution
    - Increase QR size (at least 3" × 3")
    - Add lighting to QR display area
    - Instruct guests to tap screen to focus before scanning
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="QR scans but shows error">
    **Check:**
    - Does the URL in QR match your event slug?
    - Is event active (not paused by partner)?
    - Does event have credits remaining?
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Verify QR points to correct URL (ask partner)
    - Have partner check event status
    - Top up credits if needed
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Guests can't access URL after scan">
    **Causes:**
    - Venue WiFi issues
    - Mobile data connectivity
    - Event URL malformed
    
    **Solutions:**
    - Ensure venue has strong WiFi (provide password)
    - Test with multiple phones in different areas
    - Share direct URL as backup
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="QR design doesn't show my branding">
    **Cause:**
    Partner generated QR before you updated branding in dashboard.
    
    **Solution:**
    - Update branding in your dashboard and save
    - Ask partner to regenerate QR code
    - New download includes updated logo/colors
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Need QR code urgently but partner unavailable">
    **Workaround:**
    Use free QR generator online:
    1. Go to qr-code-generator.com or similar
    2. Enter your event URL: `app.metalabia.com/?event=[slug]`
    3. Generate and download
    4. Won't have custom branding but will work
    
    **Note:** For official/branded version, still request from partner later.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

### Before Event
- 📝 Request QR code from partner at least 5 days before event
- 🖨️ Print test copy and scan with 3+ different phones
- 📍 Plan placement locations in advance
- 👥 Brief staff on how to demonstrate

### During Event
- 👀 Monitor guest engagement (are they scanning?)
- 👮‍♀️ Have backup QR codes printed (in case of damage)
- 📱 Keep direct URL handy for guests who can't scan
- 📢 Announce QR location periodically

### After Event
- 📊 Track how many guests used the booth (via gallery)
- 📝 Note what worked (placement, signage)
- ♻️ Save QR files for future reference
- 💬 Share feedback with partner
