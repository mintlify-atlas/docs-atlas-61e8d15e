# Welcome to Cabina by Creativa Labs

Cabina is an **AI-powered photo booth platform** that transforms ordinary photos into stunning AI-generated artwork in seconds. Built for both direct consumers and white-label event experiences.

## What Makes Cabina Unique?

<Note>
**Zero-Friction Events**: Guests generate AI photos without registration, login, or payment. Perfect for weddings, quinceañeras, corporate events, and parties.
</Note>

<Tip>
**Atomic Credit System**: Built-in PostgreSQL functions ensure credits are deducted atomically, preventing race conditions even during high-traffic events.
</Tip>

<Warning>
**Production Ready**: Cabina has been battle-tested at live events with 100+ concurrent users generating photos simultaneously.
</Warning>

## Platform Stats

- **50+ AI Styles**: Curated library of pre-configured Flux models
- **3-Tier Access**: Master, Partner, and Client dashboards
- **Sub-10s Generation**: Average AI photo generation time
- **100% Serverless**: No servers to manage, infinite scalability
